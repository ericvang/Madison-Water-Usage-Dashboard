This project tracks and analyzes daily water usage patterns throughout Madison, Wisconsin in 2019. We provide comprehensive data on water consumption broken down by consumer type, helping the community understand usage trends and promote conservation efforts.

Our Dataset Includes:

  • Daily water usage data from January 2019 to August 2020
  • Breakdowns by residential, multi-family, commercial, industrial, and public authority usage
  • Total gallons consumed across the city

Project Goals:

  • Monitor water consumption patterns across different sectors
  • Identify opportunities for water conservation
  • Provide transparent access to water usage data
  • Support research and policy development for sustainable water management

Explore our data browser to see the complete dataset, or consider supporting our project with a donation to help expand our research efforts.
